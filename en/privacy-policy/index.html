Privacy Policy

At Better Rail, we prioritize your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information.

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how users interact with our app, improve its functionality, and enhance the overall user experience. Google Analytics collects information such as device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage statistics. This information is used in an aggregated and anonymized form to analyze and improve the app. We don't store your favorite routes and your search history, and we don't share any of this information with third parties.

We also use Firebase Crashlytics to help us monitor and analyze app crashes and errors. When a crash or error occurs, Firebase Crashlytics may collect certain device information, crash logs, and other diagnostic details. This information is anonymized, and used solely for the purpose of identifying and resolving issues to provide you with a more stable and reliable app experience.

You always can opt out from this data collection by disabling the "Telemetry" option in the app's settings.

Better Rail Live

iOS users have the option to get live updates on a specific train ride.

For this feature to work we keep the ride details during the time of the ride in an anonymized form. We also store logs to help us identify and resolve issues with the feature.

Information from Israel Railways

All information related to station & train routes comes directly from Israel Railways.
When you search for trains schedule in the app, the request is passed directly to Israel Railways, and the sole responsibility for processing the information is at theirs.

Please refer to Israel Railways' privacy policy for more information.
